About Pizza KingDom

The year was 2001. The place, Lalbug, Dhaka. Our founder, Unus Masum, realized that his community was hungry for irresistible pizzas, served up with authentic Dhaka City hospitality. The answer was Pizza KingDom— a cozy, neighborhood hangout with a welcoming atmosphere and some of the best, most original tastes in town. It quickly became the place where family and friends came to enjoy great meals and memories, time and again. Over 20 years later, Pizza KingDom has been one of the oldest and longest-held, single-family businesses in Dhaka City. Stop by one of our many Dhaka City-area locations today and discover why Pizza KingDom is Dhaka City’s ORIGINAL Neighborhood Pizza Place!

Greek Pizza

Greek pizza is usually heavier on the sauce than the cheese. The sauce typically has a tangy tomato paste with a strong oregano flavor. It is often only topped with cheese, which is usually a mix of mozzarella and cheddar or provolone.It may feature a variety of non-Greek or Greek toppings.
